FAQ

What is Cash2?

Cash2 cryptocurrency has all the useful properties of cash but is also digital, decentralized, non-inflationary, and borderless. Compared to Bitcoin, Cash2 is fast, fungible, and has low fees.

What are the goals of Cash2?

Fast, low fees, fungible

Is Cash2 a Scam?

No. Cash2 does not have a premine, and everyone has an equal opportunity to mine, buy, and sell Cash2.

How Can I Mine Cash2?

The Innosilicon S11 and StrongU STU-U2 are recommended for mining Cash2. Please check out our YouTube channel and Discord for help with mining.

Exchanges

Is Cash2 a Privacy Coin?

No. Cash2 provides only a basic level of privacy for improved fungibility. Cash2 should not be used for illegal transactions.

Why does Cash2 use BLAKE2b instead of Cryptonight?

Fast block times requires a fast hashing algorithm, and BLAKE2b is one of the fastest algorithms available.
